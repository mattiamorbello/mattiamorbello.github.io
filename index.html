I am a PhD student under the supervision of Frank Loray, at Rennes University, since September 2022.

My research project is to understand the geometry of the Painlevé equations, in particular I am currently studying the behaviour of the foliation induced by the Riemann-Hilbert map on the boundary of the compactified moduli space of Painlevé V connections.
